# AutoStream Pricing & Features

## Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Features:** 
  - 10 videos/month
  - 720p resolution
- **Support:** Standard support.

## Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Features:** 
  - Unlimited videos
  - 4K resolution
  - AI captions
- **Support:** 24/7 support available only on Pro plan.

# Company Policies

- **Refund Policy:** We offer no refunds after 7 days of purchase.
- **Support Policy:** 24/7 premium support is exclusive to the Pro plan. Basic plan includes standard ticket-based support.
- **Sign Up:** Users can sign up by providing their Name, Email, and Creator Platform (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.).
